Meet Mimi


Mimi is a self-taught 22-year-old profesional nail tech, with three years of experience, and a wide range of artistic aesthetic.

A desire for change and a passion for creativity, Mimi started her journey after working at a full-time 9-5 job while also attending a CNA course part-time. Quickly after, Mimi recognized that the path she was leading was not meant for her. Seeking change in her life, Mimi decided to take a leap of faith during the height of the pandemic and went on to explore new opportunities through her passion for beauty.

After saving up some of her finances, she spent hours watching YouTube tutorials on nail artistry and tech skills, and in May of 2020, after ordering her first professional nail kit, she spent hours practicing on anyone willing to become her free canvas. Gradually, her love for nail art blossomed, accompanied by a growing proficiency that came with patience and dedication.
In July of 2020, she officialy launched her services to clients who were eager to experience her artistic style. Since then, her journey as a nail tech has been filled with memorable moments and satisfied clients.
